CryptocyberIntel by giottus

Intelligence for the cypto era



CryptoCyberIntel is a cutting-edge Web3
investigative tool designed and engineered by
Giottus Crypto Platform specifically for Indian law
enforcement agencies and public institutions.With
key inputs from the Cyberpeace Foundations, the
tool was built from scratch with the sole focus of
enabling governments to investigate crypto cases.



CryptoCyberIntel accesses on-chain data across
various Web3 networks simultaneously to trace
transactions and gather user data,and provides
government investigators with prosecution-ready
data.Crypto CyberIntel includes a chatbox and
service backend to help agencies to reach out to
us for support with advanced and complicated
cases.



Training Sessions



Giottus Crypto Platform and Cyberpeace Foundation
conduct joint trainings for law enforcement agencies
and public institutions across india,enabling them to
understand the wen3 ecosystem and actively use
CryptoIntel in their investigations.

CryptoIntel training sessions are day-long programs
which include the basics of Web3 and crypto,and
introduction to advanced tools and platforms.We also
provide certified training programs in partnership with
law enforcement agencies for their officers and
staffers.

Key areas of CyberPeace Foundation work are in
