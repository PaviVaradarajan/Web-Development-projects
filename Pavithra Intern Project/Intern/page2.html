CryptocyberIntel by giottus

Intelligence for the cypto era



CryptocyberIntel is a cutting-edge Web3
investigative tool designed and engineered by
Giottus Crypto Platform specifically for Indian law
enforcement agencies and public institutions.

With key inputs from the Cyberpeace Foundation,
the tool was built from scratch with the sole focus
of enabling governments to investigate crypto
cases.CryptoCyberIntel accesses on-chain data
across various Web3 networks simultaneously to
trace transactions and gather user data,and
provides government investigators with
prosecution-ready data.



CryptoCyberIntel includes a chatbox and service
backend to help agencies to reach out to us for
support with advanced and complicated cases.



Training Sessions



Giottus Crypto Platform and Cyberpeace Foundation
conduct joint trainings for law enforcement agencies
and public institutions across india,enabling them to
understand the wen3 ecosystem and actively use
CryptoIntel in their investigations.

CryptoIntel training sessions are day-long programs
which include the basics of Web3 and crypto,and
introduction to advanced tools and platforms.We also
provide certified training programs in partnership with
law enforcement agencies for their officers and
staffers.

CyberPeace Foundation is a non-profit civil society organisation and think
tank of cyber and policy experts with the vision of pioneering cyberPeace
initiatives to build collective resilience against cybercrimes & global
threats of cyber warfare.

CyberPeace Foundation's key areas of work are technology governance,
policy review,advocacy,capacity and capability creation,and building
through partnerships with various government organisations,academic
institutions, and civil society entities.
